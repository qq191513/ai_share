一、短视频分镜脚本

使用 AI语言大模型生成AI短视频脚本 ，在这里我用的是 智谱清言 。生成的分镜脚本如下：

《美女沙滩游玩的一天》

分镜1：日出时分

中文：在晨曦微弱的写实风格海滩上，一位中国美女坐在沙滩上，望着远方。她的长发随风轻轻飘动，脸上带着期待的表情。背景是渐变的橙色和粉红色天空，海浪轻轻拍打着岸边，气氛宁静而梦幻。

英文：On a beach with an Impressionist style sunrise, a Chinese girl sits on the sand, gazing into the distance. Her long hair flutters in the breeze, her face filled with anticipation. The background features a gradient of orange and pink sky, with gentle waves lapping at the shore, creating a tranquil and dreamy atmosphere.

分镜2：沙滩漫步

中文：在阳光照耀的写实风格海滩上，一位赤脚的中国美女手持贝壳，在海边漫步，低头寻找贝壳。她的脸上洋溢着轻松愉快的笑容，背景是清澈的海水和轻拍的海浪，气氛轻松而愉快。

英文：On a sunlit realistic beach, a Chinese girl walks barefoot along the shore, holding a shell, searching for more. Her face is bright with a relaxed and joyful smile. The background showcases clear seawater and softly crashing waves, creating a light-hearted and pleasant atmosphere.

分镜3：海风轻拂

中文：在写实风格的海滩上，一位赤脚的中国美女微笑着，长发随风飘动。她闭上眼睛，感受海风的轻拂。背景是海浪声和飞翔的海鸥，气氛宁静而放松。

英文：On a realistic beach, a Chinese girl smiles with her long hair fluttering in the wind. She closes her eyes, savoring the gentle sea breeze. The background is filled with the sound of waves and flying seagulls, creating a peaceful and relaxed atmosphere.

分镜4：日落时分

中文：在写实风格的海滩上，一位赤脚的中国美女望着夕阳。她坐在沙滩上，双手抱膝。背景是夕阳的余晖和轻拍的海浪，气氛温暖而怀旧。

英文：On a realistic beach at sunset, a Chinese girl gazes at the setting sun. She sits on the sand, hugging her knees. The background features the afterglow of the sunset and softly crashing waves, creating a warm and nostalgic atmosphere.

分镜5：星空下的海滩

中文：在写实风格的海滩上，一位赤脚的中国美女仰望星空。她躺在沙滩上，手指指向星空。背景是闪烁的星光和海浪声，气氛神秘而宁静。

英文：On a realistic beach under the starry sky, a Chinese girl looks up at the stars. She lies on the sand, pointing towards the starry sky. The background is filled with twinkling stars and the sound of waves, creating a mysterious and tranquil atmosphere.

分镜6：告别海滩

中文：在写实风格的海滩上，一位赤脚的中国美女回头望向海滩。她提起行李，准备离开。背景是清晨的海滩和海浪声，气氛依依不舍而期待。

英文：On a realistic beach, a Chinese girl turns back to look at the beach. She picks up her luggage, ready to leave. The background features an early morning beach and the sound of waves, creating an atmosphere of reluctance and anticipation.
